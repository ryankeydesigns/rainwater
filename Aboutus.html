About Us

Our Brand Story

At Rainwater Consultancy, we are driven by a passion for sales and a deep appreciation for the power of rainwater. Rainwater is an essential element in our lives, nourishing the earth and sustaining us. Similarly, we believe that sales is essential to the success of every business, providing the foundation for growth, innovation, and progress.

We understand that creating a desired ripple effect in sales doesn't require many actions, but rather the right action, executed with precision and clear intention. At Rainwater Consultancy, we specialize in helping businesses find the right sales and marketing strategies to achieve their goals.

Our logo serves as a visual representation of our approach to sales. The traffic light colors represent the different stages of the business life cycle, and the actions that businesses need to take to achieve success.
The Red light represents the need to stop and reflect when sales are not doing well, taking time and using data to analyze the root cause of the issue.

The Yellow light represents the importance of developing strategies, preparing, and equipping your team with the right skills and tools to execute the sales strategies. This is the time to invest in training, coaching, and development to ensure that your team is ready to execute.

Finally, the Green light represents the time to execute and measure the outcomes of your sales strategies. This is the time to put your plans into action, track your progress, and continuously refine your approach to achieve the best results possible.
